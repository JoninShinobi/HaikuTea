Privacy Policy - HaikuTea Poetry Scout Bot

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Discord bot collects and processes the following information:

  • Discord user IDs and usernames

  • Command usage patterns and timestamps

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used to:

  • Provide poetry talent scouting services
  • Process and respond to bot commands
  • Maintain service functionality and performance
  • Generate talent acquisition reports and recommendations

3. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely and accessed only as necessary to provide services. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the requested services.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

Users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data by contacting us through Discord.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes.
